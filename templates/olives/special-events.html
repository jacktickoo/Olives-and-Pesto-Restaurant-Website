Olives and Pesto's Annual food festival is back!

After the huge success of our Olives and Pesto Food and Drink Festival 2020, one of Scotland’s most prestigious food festivals, our Springfest Festival will return in 2021! We will be back on the 4th & 5th April with another fantastic line up of food and drink producers, live music, mixologist master classes and chef demonstrations. With a great line up of talent and tempting food and drink from both local producers and from across the UK next year’s festival at Loch Lomond Shores is shaping up to be our best yet!

Running from 10am to 6pm each day the festival is full of great things to see and do that will keep you busy all day. Watch top chefs cook some of their favourite recipes, enjoy a line-up of interesting and exciting entertainment, while enjoying a drink at our outside bar area.

With over 80 food and drink producers featuring everything from hot food to luxury packaged produce, festival goers can enjoy great quality food on the day and take home some tempting treats to enjoy at home.

Wine Tastings

Starting from as little as £22.50, our Glasgow Single Evening Wine Tastings are a great opportunity to try out a tasting and meet others who share your enjoyment of wine. The types of evening available are varied and always changing, from our Introduction to Wine Tasting courses and World of Wine courses to our Fine Wine Tasting Events - with plenty to choose from, there is always something new to try. Each Wine Tasting evening is suitable for anyone, from the complete beginner to the fully experienced! The price for all the one evening wine tastings include a minimum of six wines, tasting notes on the selected wines and a tutored tasting with digital projection equipment and course materials. Professional ISO tasting glasses are provided.

Cocktail Masterclass

Mixology Events’ 100% interactive, award-winning cocktail making classes in Glasgow are based at our Olives and Pesto restaurant, with the option of taking them with us anywhere across the UK. No matter how big or small your group may be, our fully-customisable cocktail making class is centred around a fully interactive experience for every single guest.
